# Electron Beam Lithography (EBL)

This repository contains my 2nd Year B.Tech seminar report on Electron Beam Lithography (EBL), a high-resolution nanofabrication technique widely used in semiconductor manufacturing, VLSI technology, 
and nanotechnology. The report explains the working principle of EBL, electron beam exposure, resist materials, pattern development, proximity effects, and etching techniques. It also highlights the advantages, 
challenges, applications, and future scope of EBL in areas such as quantum computing, nanoelectronics, photonics, MEMS/NEMS, and advanced semiconductor device fabrication.
